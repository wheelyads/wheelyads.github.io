The Problem

Static billboards are routinely ignored, and mobile digital ads are instantly skipped. Millions of commuters spend 15–25 minutes in "digital dead-time" during transit without engaging public context or local validation.

The Solution

We install ruggedized, IoT-enabled 12.5-inch smart screens directly in the back of high-frequency auto-rickshaws, creating a secure environment for localized public service and target retail campaigns.

Social & Driver Impact

Empowering our driver-partners with a sustainable 15–20% boost in recurring monthly take-home pay, providing vital economic safety nets for local urban transport families.

Proprietary "Multi-Stream" Partitioned Layout

Our balanced presentation splits the passenger's view to optimize visual retention and campaign interaction metrics simultaneously.

Zone A: Premium Video Engine (70% Screen)

Seamless, gapless looping of HD trailers and commercials via customized ExoPlayer engine.

Zone B: Interactive Conversion (20%)

Geo-fenced QR triggers for performance lead generation.

Zone C: Trust Ticker (10%)

Breaking: Hyper-local weather trends and essential real-time civic news broadcasted natively in vernacular scripts...

The Technical Architecture

Built for industrial resilience under rugged on-road variables and severe operating climates.

Precision Geo-Fencing

Campaign items trigger automatically when entering specified target boundaries (e.g., within 2km of a specific mall or theater retail cluster).

Offline-First Synchronizations

Media assets are cached cleanly in local non-volatile environments. Dynamic updates push selectively only when 4G connectivity values reach baseline metrics, cutting streaming cost structures by 80%.

Industrial Ignition-Sense Protocol

Utilizes structured 3-wire interfaces (BATT+, ACC, GND) paired with step-down converters to smoothly manage load spikes and coordinate smart sleep cycles natively with vehicle ignition states.
